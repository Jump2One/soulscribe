1. Introduction

Welcome to Soul Scribe, the mobile application (referred to as "the App") designed to help you store and cherish your memories in various formats, including images, videos, audio, and text. The App also enables you to share these memories with your friends and create memory journals to capture special moments in your life. Soul Scribe is dedicated to providing you with a platform to record significant milestones and reflections on the meaning of life. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App. By accessing or using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully and ensure that you fully understand it before using the App. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the App.

We collect and process various types of information:

    • Content: You may upload and provide content such as images, videos, audio recordings, and text when using the App.
    • Personal Details: You may choose to provide additional personal information such as your date of birth, gender, and location, though this is optional.
  • Automatically Collected Information
    • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including its unique identifier, operating system, and mobile network information.

    We may receive information about you from third-party platforms, such as social media sites, if you choose to log in to the App using your social media account.
